1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Telegram Account Data: When you log in through Telegram, we may obtain certain limited information related to your Telegram account (such as your username, phone number, profile picture) to enable the App's functionalities.
  • User Communications: Any messages, voice notes, or files you send/receive through the App are transmitted through Telegram's servers. We do not store or retain these communications on our own servers.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

  • Usage Data: We may collect information about your interactions with the App, such as which screens you visit and how long you spend in the App.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access the App, including the device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

1.3 Location Data (Optional)

When you voluntarily use the “Share Location” feature and grant permission, we temporarily access your device’s precise or approximate geographic location (e.g., GPS coordinates or network-based location). This information is used solely to create a one-time location message and is never stored on, or transmitted through, our own servers.

1.4 Third-Party Services

We may use analytics tools or other third-party services (e.g., Firebase, Google Analytics, etc.) to help us measure App traffic and usage trends. These tools may collect information sent by your device or the App, such as web pages you visit or features you use within the App, and assist us in analyzing and improving the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  1. To Provide and Maintain the App: We use your information to operate, maintain, and improve the features and functionality of the App.
  2. To Facilitate Location Sharing: If you choose to share your location, we use the location data exclusively to generate and send the requested location message to your chosen chat. We do not continue to monitor or store your location after the message has been sent.To Communicate with You: We may send you notifications, updates, and other important information related to the App.
  3. To Analyze Usage and Trends: We use usage data to understand and analyze the usage trends and preferences of our users, to improve the App, and to develop new features.
  4. To Comply with Legal Requirements: We may use or disclose your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share information in the following ways:

  1. With Telegram: Since this App is a third-party client for Telegram, certain limited information is shared with Telegram's servers, as required for functionality (e.g., delivering messages, fetching chats). Please review Telegram's Privacy Policy for further details on how they handle your personal data.

Third-Party Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors, service providers, or contractors who perform services on our behalf, such as analytics, push notifications, or crash reporting.   4. Legal Compliance and Protection: We may disclose information where we believe it is necessary to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests; to enforce or apply our rights; or to protect our users, the App, or the public from harm or illegal activities.

4. Data Retention

We do not store your messages, media, or chat data on our servers; this data is processed and stored by Telegram. Non-personal usage data we collect (e.g., analytics data) may be retained for as long as is necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described herein, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Location data generated via the “Share Location” feature is purged from local cache immediately after the message is sent and is never retained on our servers.
